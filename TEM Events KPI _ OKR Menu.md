## **How I'd frame TEM Events strategically**

For the operating plan, I'd describe Events as:  
**TEM Events creates high-value experiences that convert content, relationships, and education into measurable action — supporting participant engagement, client retention, market presence, and growth.**  
And I'd separate the two event engines:

* **Education Events** drive participant action, engagement, appointments, and financial wellness behavior.  
* **B2B Events** drive sponsor relationships, market presence, thought leadership activation, consultant/prospect engagement, and follow-up opportunities.
