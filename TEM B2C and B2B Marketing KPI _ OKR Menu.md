## **My recommendation**

For **B2C**, the scorecard should be more performance-marketing oriented:  
**reach → engagement → action → NCI / RRR / INM → segment learning.**  
For **B2B**, the scorecard should be more market-development oriented:  
**awareness → target account engagement → sales/consultant enablement → pipeline/rebid support → thought leadership activation.**  
That would give AMCE a clean distinction: **B2C Marketing moves participants; B2B Marketing moves sponsors, consultants, prospects, and market perception.**
